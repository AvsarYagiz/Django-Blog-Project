Snapshots

Geology and I have a deep connection, like a hidden love story between rock layers. I believe every rock I see has the potential for a joke. For instance, one day a sandstone said to me, 'Our bond is really tight, not as thin as sand.' Can I call that stone-hearted? Even one day a volcano took inspiration from me and said, 'I'm ready to burn with you forever!' Volcanoes truly understand real love!

If I had to choose between being an invisible geologist and living in a world filled with my earth-shattering jokes, I'd definitely go for the latter! My jokes are as deep as tectonic layers, but at least they don't come with seismic effects!
